## What is AridGuard?

AridGuard provides deterministic, automated drought protection for farmers. By leveraging the Chainlink Runtime Environment (CRE), AridGuard continuously monitors off-chain weather conditions and automatically triggers on-chain smart contract payouts when rainfall drops below a critical threshold.

**No manual claims. No delays. Just instant, transparent payouts.**

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Parametric Insurance Model">
    Unlike traditional insurance, AridGuard uses predefined parameters (rainfall below 10mm) to automatically trigger payouts. No claims adjusters, no paperwork, no delays.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Median Aggregation">
    The CRE workflow uses consensus median aggregation to ensure reliable, tamper-resistant weather data from multiple oracle nodes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="10x Coverage">
    When drought conditions are detected, farmers receive 10x their premium payment automatically to their wallet.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Base Sepolia Deployment">
    Built on Base Sepolia testnet for low-cost, fast transactions with Ethereum security.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
